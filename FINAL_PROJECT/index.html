Pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

In Italy, pizza served in a restaurant is presented unsliced, and is eaten with the use of a knife and fork. In casual settings, however, it is cut into wedges to be eaten while held in the hand.

Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

Our food delivery website operates 24/7, allowing customers to log in and place orders at any time that suits them. There is a wide selection of restaurants and delivery options available, making it easy to order food anytime.

How do i get my refund?

The customer support team will review and verify the order details and reason for the refund request, and the refund will be processed through the original payment method. Allow a few business days for the refund to reflect in the account. We are committed to providing assistance.

How long it will take food to arrive?

The delivery time can vary depending on the distance between the restaurant and your location, the preparation time of the food, and the current demand for deliveries. Generally, the delivery time is 20-30 minutes, but during peak hours or busy periods, it may be longer.

Does your restaurant has both veg and non veg?

Our restaurant offers a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options to cater to all customers. Explore our menu to discover delicious options that suit your dietary needs and preferences.

What is cost of food delivery?

The cost of food delivery varies depending on the distance between the restaurant and your location, the total order value, and any applicable delivery fees. At our food delivery website, we strive to provide transparent and competitive pricing and provide a fair and affordable service.

Who is eligible for pro membership?

Our Pro membership offers exclusive perks such as free delivery, special discounts, and priority access to promotions. Sign up today to unlock a world of benefits and enhance your food delivery experience.
